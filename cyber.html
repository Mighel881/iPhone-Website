Cybersafety is a big problem in todays society, with the rise of the internet and social networks such as Facebook, Twitter and Snapchat.
Snapchat is especially popular with teens and young adults, as it allows people to share photos that 'disappear' soon after they are posted.
However, these photos 'disappear', cyberbullies see this as a way to attack people without it getting caught. Other social media sites such as Kik have been
critisized because anyone can chat with anyone.

Facebook is known for taking away your privacy. The site will try and obtain as much infomation from you as possible.
There are settings that you can change to prevent Facebook from using some of this infomation, but not all of it. Facebook was
known to use your location to suggest people to follow. It never seeked users permission to do this, which made many
users very angry. Facebook did confess and apologise, but not after denying that they ever did it. Finally, you can no longer
create a profile that is completely private, as that toggle has been removed. Anyone can search you up on Facebook and find your profile.
